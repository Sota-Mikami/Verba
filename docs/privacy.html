Verba

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Verba is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your voice data never leaves your device.

1. Data Collection

Verba does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All voice processing happens entirely on your device using WhisperKit and Apple MLX.

2. Voice & Audio Data

Audio recordings are processed locally on your Mac. No audio data is sent to any external server. Recordings are stored only in your local Application Support directory.

3. License Verification

When you activate or validate a license key, Verba communicates with LemonSqueezy's API (api.lemonsqueezy.com) to verify your license. Only your license key and device name are transmitted. No other personal data is shared.

4. Analytics & Tracking

Verba does not include any analytics, telemetry, or tracking. No cookies are used.

5. Third-Party Services

The only external communication is license verification via LemonSqueezy. No other third-party services are integrated.

6. Open Source

Verba's source code is publicly available under the MIT License. You can audit the code at github.com/Sota-Mikami/Verba.

7. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub or contact via the repository.
