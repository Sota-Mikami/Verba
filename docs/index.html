How it works

From voice to text in seconds, entirely on your device

1

Press your shortcut

Hold fn for push-to-talk or double-tap for hands-free. A floating indicator confirms recording.

2

Speak naturally

Talk at your normal pace in any supported language. Real-time audio level feedback shows the app is listening.

3

WhisperKit transcribes

On-device speech recognition converts your audio to text using Apple's Neural Engine. Fast and accurate.

4

Text appears

Optionally formatted by an on-device LLM, the clean text is pasted directly into your active app.
