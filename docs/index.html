How it works

From voice to text in seconds, entirely on your device

1

Press your shortcut

Hold fn for push-to-talk or double-tap for hands-free.

2

Speak naturally

Talk in any language. Live text preview shows what's being recognized.

3

WhisperKit transcribes

On-device speech recognition on Apple Neural Engine. Fast and private.

4

Text appears

Optionally formatted by LLM, clean text is pasted into your active app.
